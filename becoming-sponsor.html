Becoming a sponsor

The ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing is the premier research conference on cloud computing. Attendees include top graduate students and premier experts from academia and industry. The 2017 conference will be held in Santa Clara, California in the heart of Silicon Valley, and attracts a variety of attendees from industry and universities.

We welcome sponsorship! Sponsoring the conference is an excellent way to attract potential recruits, get visibility for your company and products, and be a part of the cloud computing research community. Please contact Hakim Weatherspoon (hweather at cs dot cornell dot edu) and Hani Jamjoom (jamjoom at us dot ibm dot com) to sponsor the conference.
